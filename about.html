Meet Your Agent

Hi, I'm William Chesson

I'm the owner and Broker-in-Charge of Chesson Realty — A Maximum Service Company. Born and raised in the Raleigh area, I've spent more than two decades helping families across the Triangle find the right home and sell with confidence.

Alongside real estate, I've been a licensed North Carolina contractor for over 20 years. That builder's background gives me a sharp eye for a home's true condition and value — so you know exactly what you're getting.

I'm also a proud, honorably discharged U.S. Army veteran and a member of the 82nd Airborne Division Association. The discipline and integrity I learned in service shape how I treat every client. Family matters to me, too — I've been happily married for 30 years. When you work with me, you get an honest, hands-on partner who knows this area because it's home.
