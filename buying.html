Buyer Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How much do I need for a down payment?

It depends on your loan. Many buyers put down far less than the old "20%" rule — some programs allow 3% or even 0% for qualified buyers (VA and USDA loans, for example). Let's talk with a lender and find what fits you.

Does it cost me anything to work with you as a buyer?

In most cases, my representation as your buyer's agent costs you nothing out of pocket. We'll go over the specifics for your situation up front so there are never surprises.

I have less-than-perfect credit. Can I still buy?

Often, yes. There are many loan programs for different credit situations. The first step is a friendly chat with a lender — and I'll connect you with one who'll treat you right.

How long does buying take?

From accepted offer to keys is typically 30–45 days. Finding the right home varies — but with pre-approval in hand, we're ready to move the moment it appears.

Why does your contractor background matter to me?

Because I can spot the difference between cosmetic and costly. Before you fall for fresh paint, I'll help you understand a home's real condition — roof, systems, foundation, and more.
