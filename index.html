Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you cover?

I work throughout the Triangle and Johnston County — Raleigh, Clayton, Garner, Wendell, Cary, Apex, Smithfield, Knightdale, Fuquay-Varina, Holly Springs, Zebulon, Wake Forest and the towns in between. If you're not sure I cover your spot, just ask.

I'm a first-time buyer. Where do I start?

Start with a no-pressure conversation. We'll talk about your budget, get you connected with a trusted local lender for pre-approval, and map out exactly what to expect. Visit the Buying page for the full step-by-step.

How do you decide what my home is worth?

I combine current Triangle market data with an on-site walkthrough. My 20+ years as a licensed contractor means I can spot the features and condition details that genuinely move your price — up or down. See the Selling page.

What does it cost to work with you?

For buyers, my guidance typically costs you nothing out of pocket — let's talk specifics for your situation. For sellers, we'll go over commission and what's included up front, in plain English, before you commit to anything.

How quickly can we get started?

As soon as you're ready. Call or text me directly at 919-413-7512, or send a note from the Contact page and I'll get right back to you.
