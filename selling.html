Seller Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you decide my home's price?

I look at recent comparable sales, current Triangle market conditions, and your home's specific condition and features — which my contractor background helps me read accurately. The goal: the highest price the market will truly support.

What does it cost to sell?

We'll review commission and any costs up front, in plain English, before you commit to anything. You'll always know exactly what to expect.

Should I make repairs before listing?

Sometimes yes, sometimes no. I'll walk your home with you and recommend only the improvements that pay you back — and tell you honestly which ones won't.

How long will it take to sell?

It depends on price, condition, and the market, but well-prepared, well-priced homes in our area often move quickly. I'll give you a realistic timeline for your specific situation.

Can you help if I'm buying and selling at the same time?

Absolutely — I do this often. We'll coordinate the timing so the transition is as smooth and stress-free as possible.
